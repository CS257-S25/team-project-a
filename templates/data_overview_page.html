Data Overview

How to read the graph

This graph plots the number of self-help meetings for drug abuse participants went to versus the self-reported effect of drugs on their mental health. The opacity of the dots on the chart corresponds to the number of respondents who had that combo of responses. Finally, the self-reported effect of drugs on the respondent's mental health was rated on a scale from 1 - 5 which can be seen in the chart.
